🪑 Why Do Bed Bugs Infest Offices & Commercial Spaces?

Bed bugs are opportunistic. They don't care if it's a luxury condo or a busy accounting office. All they need is warmth, fabric, and access to people. In fact, commercial spaces can be prime environments for infestations:

  • Shared breakrooms, couches, and waiting areas
  • High foot traffic from clients, guests, and staff
  • Personal belongings brought from infested homes
  • Limited nightly activity, allowing bugs to spread undetected

From single-unit businesses to multi-floor office buildings, no workplace is immune—and ignoring the problem only makes it worse.
